Recipe: Lasagne

The traditional Albanian dish Fli is prepared in a special way. A cover plate is heated over a fire pit. Coal is taken from the fire pit and the cover plate is covered with it. This way a high temperature is achieved. The cover plate is then placed over the baking tray and the contents are baked.

    Ingredients:

  1. 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  2. 1 pound ground beef chuck
  3. 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  4. 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  7. 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  8. 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  9. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  10. 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf), packed
  11. 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  12. 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  13. 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  14. 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
  15. Salt

    Steps:

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
